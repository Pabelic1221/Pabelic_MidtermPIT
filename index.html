ABOUT US

Anchored on the spirituality of Mother Ignacia del Espiritu Santo, the school implements the Ignacian Core Values Framework. Featured in a wheel, the framework synthesizes what the school believes to be the essential elements in the formation of Ignacian spirituality.

A PAASCU accredited school, Lourdes College is one of the oldest and distinguished Catholic institutions in Southern Philippines known for its tradition of excellence and unwavering commitment to provide quality Christian education. With Faith, Excellence and Service as its core values, this school draws inspirations from the ideals and virtues of Venerable Ignacia del Espiritu Santo and the Blessed Virgin Mary. These core values, translated into the school vision and mission, are integrated in the programs and practices that promote the holistic formation of students.
